As of February 2014 our agenda/travel plans for the next six months
As with most cruiser plans they are all subject to change but our best guess is.  Leave Puerto Rico by the end of February and spend Derek's birthday in the Bahamas.  Spend March/April cruising north and hit the water park of Atlantis in Nassau.  Head over to the US coast, not sure if it will be Florida or South Carolina but then eventually spend a month at a marina working on the boat.  In July haul the boat and then travel via land back to the west coast and visit family and friends.  In August finish up any boat projects and put the boat back in the water so we can avoid any hurricanes.
