We had scheduled the open boat for a nice sunny day in June.  We moved the boat to the transient dock, and got it all prettied up for our guests.  The marina was having a "parrot head" event going on, so we anticipated live music and fun.

What we got, was cold cold cold weather, with rain and wind.   Oh well, can't win them all.

We ended up having a great time, with 43 people stopping by to say hi and see "What If".  The boat made a good showing of itself, by staying nice and dry and warm below, and seemed quite cozy - especially when we had 13 people at one time all inside.

We would like to thank everyone who braved the weather and made it down to the boat, we really enjoyed visiting with you all.
